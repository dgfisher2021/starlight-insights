Aries: The Celestial Origin Point

The Universe's Ultimate Reference Point

March 21 - April 19

The Science

Aries contains the "First Point of Aries" - the zero point of celestial coordinates that defines the entire coordinate system on the celestial sphere. This point marks where the Sun crosses the celestial equator during the spring equinox, making it the fundamental reference point for astronomical navigation.

The Meaning

You're naturally wired to be the pathfinder, the one who sets the direction for everyone else. Like that cosmic reference point that defines where everything else begins, you don't just start things—you define the very foundation from which all other journeys begin.

Message for You

When others are lost in space (literally or figuratively), you're the cosmic compass pointing true north. Trust your natural ability to see the bigger picture and guide others toward their destinations. You're not just a leader—you're the original blueprint for leadership itself.

Taurus: The Magnificent Rebirth

A Supernova Explosion Visible in Daylight

April 20 - May 20

The Science

Taurus contains the Crab Nebula (M1), a supernova remnant from an explosion witnessed by Chinese astronomers in 1054 AD. This expanding nebula was bright enough to be visible during the day and is now one of the most studied objects in astronomy.

The Meaning

You are the master of magnificent rebirth, turning every ending into a spectacular new beginning. Like that ancient star that didn't just die but exploded into cosmic glory, you understand that true transformation comes through complete renewal.

Message for You

You have the power to transform life's most challenging moments into your greatest masterpieces. Don't just endure difficulties—emerge reborn, more beautiful and powerful than before. Every setback is your setup for an even more magnificent comeback.

A Five-Planet Family with Earth-Like Conditions
